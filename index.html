Power Learn Project (PLP)

A Pan-African social impact organization training developers and growing tech talent.

About PLP

PLP’s mission is to unlock Africa’s potential by training developers through accessible tech education, community, and opportunities. The vision includes the #1MillionDevs4Africa movement, building skills and jobs across the continent.

  • Programs and community: Coffee & Code, AI Safari, and more.
  • Focus: democratize tech education, bridge the skills gap, and drive youth employment.
  • Ecosystem: training, mentorship, employability support, and talent connections.
